"Who The Hell Are Sleepy Gonzales?"

          "It's not something I fully understand myself, to be honest. But it's the one thing that doesn't upset me when I picture doing it for my entire life. Sometimes, when we're playing, the sound reverberates through my body at exactly the right frequency, and in those moments, everything becomes unabatedly clear to me. Then I look over, and see the exact same look in their eyes. Nothing beats that."

          "Am I absolutely sure we can do it? No - but what would be the fun in that? The only thing I know is that one day I will look back, and be certain that we did our best. That's enough. We're just guessing, after all. Wading through the fog, unsure of what to do or where to go. It might seem like a long shot, but somehow, we all ended up in the same place, at the same time, chasing the same dream."
